Our Mission

About the Campaign

Why This Campaign Exists

The #ResignMamdani campaign was created by concerned citizens who demand that elected officials unequivocally denounce terrorism and violence without equivocation or ambiguity. In the case of Assemblymember Zohran Mamdani, his family's longstanding ties to anti-Israel activism—often criticized as tacit support for terrorism—combined with his wife's recent likes of social media posts celebrating the October 7, 2023, Hamas attacks, have eroded public confidence and compromised the principles of inclusive, safe governance in our diverse city.

This effort isn't about censorship—it's about insisting on transparency, ethical integrity, and resolute leadership from those in power. Every resident deserves representatives free from associations that sympathize with terror, and we all share the duty to ensure accountability for the sake of our community's future.

Civic Engagement

We encourage respectful, fact-based discussion about the responsibilities of public officials. This is a civic movement rooted in democratic values. We welcome people of all backgrounds to participate in constructive dialogue about accountability in public office.

Spread the Word

We encourage everyone to share the #ResignMamdani hashtag on social media, discuss the issue with friends, family, and communities, and help amplify the call for accountability. Every share, every conversation, and every voice matters in building a movement for responsible governance.
